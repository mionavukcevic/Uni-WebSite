FON students were awarded the prestigious Saint Sava Award for 2021

FON Case Study Club students: Zorana Dulović, Sergej Stanišić, Dragan Stojanović, Tamara Đorđević and Mina Petrović became the winners of the Saint Sava Award for 2021 for winning first place in the world competition in solving a case study in the field of circular economy for textile industry: Global Circular Challenge 2021.


This prestigious award, given by the Ministry of Education, Science and Technological Development, in recognition of the contribution to quality education and upbringing, improvement of educational practice and development of scientific achievements in the field of education in the Republic of Serbia was solemnly presented by First Deputy Prime Minister and Minister Branko Ruzic January 27, 2022 in the Government of the Republic of Serbia. The ceremony of awarding the Saint Sava Award was also attended by prof. Dr. Milan Martic, Dean of FON
