OUR FACULTIES

Our best ranked faculties

School of Electrical Engineering (ETF)

University of Belgrade's School Of Electrical Engineering (ETF) is the oldest institution of its kind in Serbia. How the ETF was established and how it developed can be found on the history page.

Architecture

ARCHITECTURE (ARHITEKTURA) is an one-year Master programme (60ECTS) of academic studies offered by the Faculty of Technical Sciences in Novi Sad.

Faculty of Civil Engineering

For almost half a century, the Faculty of Civil Engineering and Architecture of Niš has been educating professionals engaging in planning and arrangement of cities, in designing and construction of buildings, roads, railroads, bridges, dams and other engineering and architectural structures.

Faculty of Organizational Sciences (FOS)

FOS is a leading faculty in the field of management and information systems and technologies, with more than 12.000 graduates, 3.000 students with master’s degree, more than 600 specialists, 600 with MSc degree, and more than 300 students with PhD degree.

Faculty of Medicine

School of Medicine was opened December 9, 1920. by speech of professor Milan Jovanovic-Batut in the hall of the University. After that, the access lecture by Dr. Nick Miljanić, a professor of anatomy, marked the start of classes at the Faculty. That date since 1970. has been celebrated as the Day School.

Faculty of Sports and Physical Education

The capacities of the Faculty for teaching are: two large halls (basketball, volleyball, handball, indoor soccer), a small hall (rhythmic gymnastics and dance), a gymnasium (sports gymnastics), two amphitheaters, seven modern classrooms, a diagnostic center and a computer center . In the building on the Student Playground: a large hall, a martial arts hall and a fitness room. There are athletics arenas, tennis courts, two handball courts, a basketball court and a volleyball court.

OUR FACILITIES

Within each of our universities there are facilities that our students can use.

Library

Research has shown that the more a student engages with the library, the greater likelihood of them getting a higher grade degree. We've gathered together all the key information we think you'll need to get started and we're running a series of welcome workshops to help you get your studies on track.

Sport playground

Physical activity at a school playground plays a critical role in improving the attention spans of students, decreases stress and anxiety, and prepares the little ones for classwork. It also improves motivation and helps kids try out things that they may not be inclined to try in a classroom setting.

Canteen

The restaurants are usually located in the vicinity of student dormitories, but there are also restaurants, outside student dormitories. There are also commercial restaurants, where students may purchase meals with their student cards, the same way as in the student restaurants.In addition, these restaurants offer a wide range of foods and beverages high quality, both for students and teachers, and for all other visitors, at affordable prices.
